# Tempo Workout Engine

A lightweight, high-performance browser-based workout timer with voice coaching and interval control.

## Features
- Custom workout routines
- Interval timer (work + rest)
- Audio cues and speech synthesis
- Mobile-friendly design

## Getting Started
Open `src/index.html` in your browser from the repo root.

## Next Steps
Keep improving the app in `src/` and track larger plans in `docs/roadmap.md`.
